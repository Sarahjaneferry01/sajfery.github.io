FLOWERS

Sunflowers: Positivity and Abundance

Sunflowers are a cheerful choice, symbolizing optimism, happiness, and vitality.

Chrysanthemums:
Longevity and Joy

A traditional flower in many cultures, chrysanthemums are associated with happiness and long life.

Known for their large, trumpet-like blooms, amaryllis flowers signify success and beauty.
